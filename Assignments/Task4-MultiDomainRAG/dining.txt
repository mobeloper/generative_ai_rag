The Bella Vista Hotel has three restaurants:
- The Olive Tree (Italian, open 11am-10pm)
- The Lotus Pond (Pan-Asian, open 12pm-11pm)
- The Terrace Grill (Continental, open 6pm-11pm)
Buffet breakfast is served daily from 7am to 10am.
Room service is available 24 hours.
Sunday brunch is from 11am to 3pm.
